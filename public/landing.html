Our Mission

The development of the CJudge software platform is aimed to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly solution for writing code, compiling it, and debugging it in an online environment. The platform was designed to address the challenges of portability and storage space, and leverage the benefits of cloud computing.

Our Plan

CJudge integrated with online learning platforms and provided access to coding challenges, practice problems, and learning resources. Users could solve coding questions from different platforms within the CJudge environment, allowing for comprehensive learning and skill development.

Our Vision

By providing a comprehensive online platform, CJudge empowers developers and learners to write, test, and refine their code efficiently while facilitating collaboration, mentorship, and continuous learning within a supportive community environment.
